Hi, I'm Justin Ivey

I've Always Loved Family Adventures

I love camping. I’ve always enjoyed hitting the road when I was a kid and exploring new places.

Sleeping in a tent, cooking food, and going on new adventures with my brothers were some of the best parts of growing up.

Now as a dad with three girls of my own, I want to provide the same type of experiences for them. Connecting to nature and exploring new places helped shaped the way I looked at and interacted with the world.

My goal is to help other dads and parents find new places to explore with their kids. I’ll be sharing my experiences and tips for camping, hiking, and exploring the outdoors with kids.

With that in mind, I want to make sure that each weekend counts.

Come join us on our adventures! Let’s get out there!
